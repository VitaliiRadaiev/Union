On Thursday, November 14, UNION and Inga Kompaniets were pleased to present an exhibition showcasing a series of selected art inspired by the shared theme of humanity.

The goal was to bring together people of different professions to discuss important topics concerning the workplace environment and how each person defines comfort in the workplace. The key to design is to look at projects through the eyes of people who will be using the space every day.
