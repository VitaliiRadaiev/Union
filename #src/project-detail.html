To cook a delicious dish you need certain steps and ingredients. A delicious dish is the main inspiration for this project. The entire office has specific functions such as kitchen and tasting area, supermarket area, multimedia center, big pantry connected to training rooms for town hall meetings, photo corners.

Front of house and reception represents Kraft Heinz branding and offering an unforgettable cooking experience offline and online. The kitchen is designed to bring people together in the office and to create a cooking TV show.

The main idea for the design of meeting rooms is based on the scale of the room: small rooms are designed based on cooking ingredients while medium-scale rooms show Chinese cuisines with main dishes and cultural elements, big meeting rooms and boardrooms are representing branding of the company.

All collaborative spaces are designed with a selection of neutral colors and shapes to enhance vivid design expression of meeting rooms.
